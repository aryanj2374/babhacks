Anti-Scalp Tickets

Event tickets as NFTs with price caps, resale limits, and automatic royalties — powered by XRPL.

ℹ️ This demo runs on XRPL Testnet. All wallets and tokens are for testing only.
1
Setup Wallets & RLUSD
Create 3 funded wallets (Organizer, Fan 1, Fan 2) on XRPL Testnet and distribute simulated RLUSD stablecoin. Uses Testnet Faucet, TrustSet, and Payment transactions.
2
Mint Ticket NFTs
Organizer mints an event ticket as an NFT using NFTokenMint (XLS-20). Metadata includes event info, price, max resale price, and resale limits. TransferFee (10%) ensures automatic royalties on every resale.
3
Buy Ticket (Primary Sale)
Fan 1 purchases the ticket from the Organizer. Uses NFTokenCreateOffer (sell) + NFTokenAcceptOffer for atomic NFT↔RLUSD swap.
4
Resell Ticket (Anti-Scalping Demo)
Test the anti-scalping enforcement. Try to resell above the max price (will be blocked!), then resell at a valid price. TransferFee royalty is automatically paid to the organizer by the XRPL protocol.
5
Verify Ticket Ownership
Check who owns a specific ticket NFT and validate its metadata. Uses account_nfts RPC to query the ledger directly.
